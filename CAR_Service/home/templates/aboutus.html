About Us

Tar Car Service is one of the world's largest independent workshop chains. Our international service network encompasses more than 15,000 authorized workshops in 150 countries.

With our wide range of expert services, we can offer everything you could possibly need for your car and car service - from inspection, periodic maintenance, accidental repairs, replacement of genuine auto parts, value added services that puts and extra smile on your face.

In India, we have over 270 Tar Car Service workshops, so you are sure to find one local to you who will be happy to assist you.

Pillars of Tar Car Service

Legacy of 100 years of Tar Car Service

Since 1921 Tar Car Service has grown to become one of the largest workshop networks in the world.

World class for 100 years!

The network is now made up of carefully selected garages that meet the strict qualification requirements of Bosch and have a passion for cars and deliver great quality service. Tar Car Service has been guided by Robert Tar’s driving principles of quality and customer focus, ensuring that the high-quality standards always run throughout the business.

For everything your car needs

The claim “for everything your car needs” is a brand claim that is echoed throughout Tar Car Service. Over the past 100 years Tar Car Service has developed to become an all-round workshop network providing car drivers with high-quality vehicle maintenance, repair, and diagnosis.
